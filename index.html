The Map

Important Locations

The Moss Grotto

  • Starting point of the game.
  • You get introduced to Hornet's 45 degree down slash
    (didnt know I had to do math mid confrentation...).
  • Enemies are extremely easy, but act as a good intro!
  • Very pretty, I love the overgrown theme.
  • Moss Mother... exists...

The Marrow

  • You fight the Bell Beast (w boss).
  • You also fight the Skull Tyrant (not a W boss)
  • The Flea Caravan is... there... (I prefered the grubs).
  • Acts as a passageway to other areas.

The Deep Docks

  • Basically The Marrow, but a little more steampunk.
  • Honestly... forgettable...
  • You fight Lace pt. 1 and the forebrothers.
  • The enemies are kind of cute so thats nice!

The Far Fields

  • Temple of the Beast, but WHO is using that form.
  • You fight the Savage Beastfly (omg NEVER again).
  • Theres that one quest with the pins that makes
    you THINK you glitched but you need to get the
    balloon cloak to leave...

Greymoor

  • Reminded me of Stilt Village from Dead Cells.
  • You fight Moorwing (Top 5 Silksong bosses.).
  • The Halfway House was super cozy, I'd never leave.
  • I feel like a 24 hour long video of Hornet on a
    Greymoor bench would actually get me to study.

Shellwood

  • Shellwood is Greymoor and Moss Grotto's baby.
  • You fight Sister Splinter, Nyleth, and Seth.
    (Genuinely who are Nyleth and Seth what...)
  • Um, why are there floating flowers that EXPLODE?
  • I spent like MAYBE 2 seconds here.

The Blasted Steps

  • A more whimsical version of Jedha from Star
    Wars: Jedi Survivor.
  • You fight the Last Judge (more like my last straw...).
  • How do you get to the Sands of Karak from here??
  • Daily hurricanes.

The Citadel

  • The way they seperated the Citadel map drove me
    INSANE.

The Slab

  • Why did I get teleported here midway through Act 1?

Mount Fay

  • Mothwing cloak my BELOVED.
